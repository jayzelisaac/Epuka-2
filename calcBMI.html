Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on your weight in relation to your height. It is used as a screening tool to categorize individuals into different weight status categories.

Understanding your BMI is important for assessing your overall health and identifying potential health risks associated with being underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI Stats Table
BMI Category BMI Range Health Risk
Underweight <18.5 Increased
Normal weight 18.5-24.9 Least
Overweight 25-29.9 Increased
Obese Class I 30-34.9 High
Obese Class II 35-39.9 Very High
Obese Class III ≥40 Extremely High

Note: This table is based on the World Health Organization (WHO) classification of BMI categories. However, BMI is just one of many health parameters and should not be used as the sole indicator of health. It's always a good idea to consult with a healthcare professional to determine the best approach for managing your health.
